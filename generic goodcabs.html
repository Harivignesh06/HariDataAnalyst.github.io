Project Summary

Goodcabs, a cab service company established two years ago, has been expanding its operations in tier-2 cities across India. Unlike many competitors, Goodcabs focuses on empowering local drivers, enabling them to earn a stable income while providing quality transportation services. With operations in 10 tier-2 cities, the company has set ambitious performance targets for 2024, emphasizing growth and customer satisfaction.
